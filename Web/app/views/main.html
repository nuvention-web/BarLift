BarLift uses your location to send you relevant, high quality deals when you're out at night.

Drink specials and happy hour deals are great, but we don’t always receive that information at the right time. Oftentimes we either forget, or simply have no knowledge about the specials around us.

BarLift solves this problem by providing relevant, location-based deals that are delivered to you in real-time throughout the night when you’re actually making decisions about where to go, giving you spontaneous options at unbeatable prices. By providing bars with a mechanism to dynamically lower prices throughout the night, BarLift presents users with deals that previously never would have existed.

How does it work?

1. Dynamic Deal Creation

BarLift enables bars to instantaneously create and send out promotions to BarLift users within its vicinity.

2. Smartphone Notification

BarLift alerts users of high quality deals at nearby bars and clubs via push notifications on their smartphones.

3. Redemption

BarLift users present promotion at the bar for redemption.
